Eifell Tower

The Eiffel tower (French: La Tour Eiffel) is an iron lattice tower locaded in Champ de Mars, Paris. It was constructed in 1889 and since then has become an icon of France since, as well as one of the tallest known buildings in the world. The tower is 324 meters high, that is equivalent to approximately 81 storey building. When the tower was opened, it became the tallest building in the world, surpassing the Washington monument.
